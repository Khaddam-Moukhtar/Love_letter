To the One I Adore

A small letter, from my heart to yours — read slowly, hold close.

My khokha,

From the very first moment our paths crossed, something in me rearranged — the ordinary colors grew warmer, the quiet seconds became music. You are the calm in my busiest days and the bright thought at the edge of every evening.

I love the way you smile when you don't think anyone is watching, the way your voice turns small troubles into stories, and how simply being near you makes everything feel possible. I promise to listen, to try, to laugh with you until our ribs hurt, and to stand beside you through every small and enormous thing life sends our way.

If I could give you the sky, I would. For now I give you this—the truth of my heart, steady and honest. Thank you for being my favorite place to be.

Always yours,
— Mokhtar
